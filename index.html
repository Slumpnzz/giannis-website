The Unrelenting Climb

2013: The Draft

A raw prospect from Greece is selected 15th overall by the Milwaukee Bucks.

2014: All-Rookie

Named to the NBA All-Rookie Second Team after showing flashes of immense potential.

2016: "Point Giannis"

Begins to handle primary playmaking duties, showcasing his unique versatility. Records his first career triple-double.

2017: The Arrival

Wins Most Improved Player, makes his first All-Star and All-NBA teams, and leads the Bucks in all five major stats.

2019: The MVP

Crowned NBA's Most Valuable Player for the first time, leading the Bucks to 60 wins and the Eastern Conference Finals.

2020: Historic Double

Wins his second straight MVP and his first Defensive Player of the Year award, joining Jordan and Olajuwon in an elite club.

2021: The Pinnacle

Wins All-Star Game MVP, leads the Bucks to an NBA Championship, and is named Finals MVP after a legendary 50-point clincher.

2022: Franchise Scorer

Passes Kareem Abdul-Jabbar with a game-winning three-pointer to become the Milwaukee Bucks' all-time leading scorer.

2023: All-Around Leader

Becomes the franchise's all-time leader in assists, solidifying his status as the greatest player in Bucks history.

2025: Enduring Dominance

Continues his historic run as a 9-time All-Star, constantly in the MVP conversation and aiming for more championships.
